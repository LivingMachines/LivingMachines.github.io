About
The most powerful technology is built with people, not just for them.

Living Machines Media works at the convergence of emerging technology and media — helping organizations navigate AI, immersive reality, and new digital paradigms with intention and integrity.

Our approach is grounded in the belief that technology should amplify human creativity, protect individual agency, and distribute opportunity broadly. We bring a cooperative ethos to every engagement — informed by real experience building within the worker-owned tech ecosystem.
